Fixed Y Axis Max Values ([yBarMax]="500" & [yLineMax]="300")

Use [yBarMax] to pin the maximum of the left (bar) Y axis and [yLineMax] to pin the maximum of the right (line) Y axis. Useful to compare charts side-by-side on a consistent scale.

Y Axis Clipping – Values Exceed the Maximum ([yBarMax]="200" & [yLineMax]="100")

When data points exceed the configured maximum, they are visually clipped at the upper boundary (SVG clipPath). The actual values remain unchanged – the tooltip still shows the real value.
